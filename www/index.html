About Memos

Memos is an app meant to help you memorise practically any information by
1) Writing memo cards.
2) Regularly repeating them.
Sounds simple and it is simple.
However there's some more things to know.
First, as a user you store your memo cards in catalogs.
They are just like folders in your filesystem.
Create your first catalog by opening controls panel
⚙️ Show controls
Then, tap
📁 Add catalog
Choose an icon, a simple emoji would do.
Choose a name, like French.
Last, but not least, the fields, or the sides of your memo card.
For French example, they could be Original, Translation, Transcription.
Now you see that memo cards in Memos can have more than two sides.
After that in your file browser, go to your new French dir and add some cards!
Or at least one.
Tap
📝 Add card
Fill in the required fields (at last 2).
Then just quit by tapping ← (arrow back).
You're ready to go!

Memos repeat

Tap the circle to enter the repeating mode.
When see a card with a text (for example, Un renard)
type the translation you filled in before and that's it!
Memos will double the waited time, so you repeat the card more rarely when you know it.
However, if you don't remeber, memos will nullify the full interval so you begin fresh headed.

Memos card

Properties
⌛ Next repeat in
⏰ When in need of repeat, waiting time start counting
🔋 Integrity, if waiting time is more than expected, it will go up to 100% and more, do not allow this
💪 Time it stood still, for example 2 days without repeat 🚗 Current interval expected for the card to hold

Repeat queue

When cards need repeat, they go to repeat queue. It consist of two parts.
Children - cards whose interval is lower than N, by default 1 hour.
It's made so that you repeat one child until it becomes adult, and not all the kids you have.
Lets you focus on it.

Adult cards. Just repeat them.

Queue priority by default is
First cards by maximal integrity (biggest first).
It means memos will give you cards in highest need of repeat first.

Memos Circle

Circle has 5 fields.
1) Time before next card needs repeat.
2) Cards repeated today | Cards needs repeat | Cards will be needed to repeated today
3) % of all cards that not in need of repeated
4) Cards that ran out of its waiting time and probably dead | % of integirty of the first card in queue, better be < 100% | Average % of integirty among cards in repeat queue
5) Hours you may earn if complete all cards in queue
Also two lights bulbs.
Left one displays how many cards in queue in need of repeat.
Green -> Red (Red if you better start repeating at last).
Right displays the median interval of all cards.
If Red, it means all the cards are strong and you now should add more.

Other stuff.
You can manipulate your cards and catalogs, move and edit them easily.
Change the order of displayment and search for a card.
Also, do not forget to regularly do your backups!
Some of features are still in development, so please be patient, tell your friends
